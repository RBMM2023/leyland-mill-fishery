Prices

As Leyland Mill is for members, we ask for a £100 joining fee which automatically renews if you come within 12 months of your first visit. (If you have not revisited our fishery within the 12 months you will be required to renew your membership of another £100 fee.)

We then charge £30 for 24 hours from. These prices are inclusive of the summerhouses on pegs one and three. Fishing times are 2pm-2pm during winter and 3pm-3pm during summer.

A guest can be brought with you for an extra £15 (£10 for a junior guest- under 14.).

We have a £5 charge for use of the oil filled radiator.

We also stock a range of different baits and tackle so feel free to ask the bailiff for availability and prices on these!
